Cookies on the Get approval to use a .gov.uk domain name service

Cookies

Small files (known as 'cookies') are put onto your computer to collect information about how you browse the site.

Cookies are used to:

  • measure how you use the website so it can be updated and improved based on your needs
  • remember the notifications you've seen so that you're not shown them again
The cookies on this service are not used to identify you personally.

Find out more about how to manage cookies (opens in a new tab) .

Essential cookies

We use some essential cookies to make this service work.

Essential cookies keep your information secure while you use this service. We do not need to ask permission to use them.

Types of essential cookies
Name Purpose Expires
sessionid Used to save your session data 14 days
csrftoken Prevents submitting invalid data 1 year

Government services and external websites

Most services we link to are run by different government departments, or external websites for example Government Digital Service (GDS) or the Nominet website. These services may set additional cookies and, if so, will have their own cookies policy.
